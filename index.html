About RFC

Reno Firearms Concierge (RFC) was founded to bring a discreet, professional, and educational approach to firearms ownership. Whether you're new to firearms or seeking a premium-level acquisition experience, we serve as your trusted advisor and concierge.

Our Services

  • Personalized firearm purchase guidance
  • Private transfer and legal compliance support
  • Secure storage consultation
  • Custom concierge experiences and sourcing rare firearms

FAQ

Do you sell firearms?
No, RFC does not sell firearms. We act as your trusted consultant and connect you with licensed dealers.

Is this service legal?
Yes, RFC operates in compliance with Nevada and federal firearms laws. We do not handle transfers directly without proper licensing.

Can you help with CCW or training?
Yes, we work with vetted instructors and legal experts to provide referrals or host private training experiences.
